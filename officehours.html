Office Hours

The Ignite team are available on a regular basis to talk about a range of startup-related topics.

If you’re looking for feedback on your idea, advice on raising funding, how to hire staff, or looking to get more information on any of our programmes, you can book a slot with us to talk about it via video chat every Thursday.

It helps to have someone else to talk to – and we’re here to chat!
